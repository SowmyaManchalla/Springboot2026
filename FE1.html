Sample article

This is a sample article to demonstrate the use of the <article> tag in HTML. The <article> element is used to represent a self-contained composition in a document, page, application, or site, which is intended to be independently distributable or reusable.

Articles can contain headings, paragraphs, images, videos, and other content. They are often used for blog posts, news stories, forum posts, and other types of content that can stand alone.
